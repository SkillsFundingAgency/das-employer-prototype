{{ pageTitle }}

Ask your training provider to add apprentice details for one of more apprentices.

Funding

You can choose which funding to use. If you don’t have any funding set up, you will automatically use your current funds.

Permissions

You can change your permissions settings so your training provider can add apprentices for you, without you needing to send a request. You’ll still need to approve requests sent to you.

What you'll need

You must:

  • have a training provider
  • have authority to act for the organisation that's named on the contract you have with your training provider (view your organisations)
  • make sure the PAYE scheme that the apprentices will be paid through is added to your account (view your PAYE schemes)
