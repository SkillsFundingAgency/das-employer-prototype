How reserved funds work

You can reserve funds up to 3 months in advance or within one month of the start date.

This will cover 100% of your apprenticeship training and assessment costs if the apprentice is 22 or under, or 95% if over 22 (up to the funding band maximum).

You can authorise your training provider to reserve funds on your behalf.

How transfer funds work

You can apply to receive funds that levy-paying businesses offer to other businesses.

If your application is successful, this will cover 100% of your apprenticeship training and assessment costs (up to the funding band maximum).

How direct transfer funds work

You can arrange for a levy-paying business that you know to transfer funds directly to you, but they must invite you to do this from their employer account.

If your application is successful, this will cover 100% of your apprenticeship training and assessment costs (up to the funding band maximum).
